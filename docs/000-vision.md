# Vision

DPS is a compiler for product demonstrations.

Its source inputs are product facts, configuration, evidence, constraints, and human decisions. Its intermediate representation is the DIR. Its targets may include Remotion projects, MP4 files, vertical variants, judge cuts, subtitles, posters, and audit reports.

The core does not generate persuasion from nothing. It constructs understanding from observable product behavior.

## Primary user

A builder who has a working product but lacks the time, directing method, or production infrastructure required to create a strong demonstration repeatedly.

## MVP success condition

Given a small product manifest and verified evidence references, DPS produces a valid DIR whose selected Hero Interaction and narrative order are understandable and inspectable before rendering begins.
