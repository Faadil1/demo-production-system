# Design Laws

1. Every demo has one primary Hero Interaction.
2. Every scene declares a purpose.
3. Every motion decision declares a justification.
4. Authentic captures remain the canonical product evidence.
5. Generated visuals illustrate concepts; they never prove product behavior.
6. Important creative decisions pass explicit gates.
7. Artifacts are versioned, hashed, and attributable.
8. Engines communicate through contracts and artifacts.
9. Provider names never leak into the core domain model.
10. A scene unsupported by the Demo Graph is removed.
11. All variants derive from one canonical DIR.
12. QA is part of production, not a final optional step.
